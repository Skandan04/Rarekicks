Privacy & Policy

How Rare Kicks collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you shop for premium sneakers and luxury accessories.

Effective date: 01 August 2025

This Privacy & Policy explains how Rare Kicks (“we”, “us”, “our”) handles the personal data of customers who browse, shop, and interact with our store — whether purchasing limited-edition sneakers, luxury brand footwear, or premium accessories like caps, belts, and bags. By using our website, you agree to the terms described here.

1. Information We Collect

Details You Provide

  • Account data (name, email, password)
  • Order details for sneakers or accessories (models, sizes, quantities)
  • Shipping & billing information
  • Messages sent to our customer care team

Data Collected Automatically

  • Browsing history (e.g., viewed shoe models, brand pages)
  • Cart activity (items added, removed, or saved for later)
  • Device and IP address for security purposes

From Trusted Partners

  • Payment processors (transaction status)
  • Shipping carriers (delivery status and tracking)
  • Analytics tools to understand customer interest in specific brands

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver sneaker and accessory orders efficiently.
  • To suggest related luxury items (e.g., matching bags or caps for footwear purchases).
  • To inform you about restocks of popular sneaker sizes.
  • To run exclusive luxury product launches and VIP sales.
  • To send important updates about your order status and delivery.

3. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to remember your preferred shoe sizes, keep track of your cart across visits, and show you accessories that complement your footwear choices. You can disable cookies in your browser, but some store features may not work as intended.

4. Sharing & Disclosure

We may share your information with:

  • Shipping companies to deliver your footwear and accessories.
  • Payment partners to securely process your transactions.
  • Brand partners in case of warranty or special requests.

We never sell your personal details to third parties.

5. Data Security

Rare Kicks uses secure payment gateways and encrypted data storage to keep your information safe, whether you’re buying sneakers, designer handbags, or other luxury accessories.
